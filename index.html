Viewer Help

Camera: Left drag rotate, right drag pan, mouse wheel zoom, WASD move. On touch: one-finger drag rotate, two-finger drag pan, and pinch zoom.

Focus: Double-click or double-tap a scene point to focus camera there.

Measure Mode: Enable Measure, then single-click to add points to a polyline. Each click creates a new point; segment distances and interior angles are shown automatically. Right-click (mouse) or a quick two-finger tap (touch) finalizes the current polyline. Turning off Measure also finalizes any pending polyline.

Measure Tools: Use Del Last to remove the latest measurement. Drag a gizmo axis to move a specific point; right-click drag will not finalize — only a quick right-click (no drag) or two-finger tap finalizes. If there is no pending polyline, right-click or two-finger tap cycles selection through the points of the last measurement.

Move Slider: Drag the bottom-left vertical slider up/down for forward/backward movement. Releasing it returns speed to neutral.

Cut Tool: Enable Cut Tool and choose Plane or Box. Plane: drag a colored axis line to move or axis tip to rotate. Box: drag RGB axis lines to move along world X/Y/Z, drag local axis tips to rotate, and drag yellow face handles to move each face along its normal.

Tips: Click directly on the visible scene to place points. Small accidental pointer movement during point placement will not rotate the camera — only click-and-drag will rotate. Dragging (hold + move) after selection rotates the camera; use quick taps to place points.
