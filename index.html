Viewer Help

Camera: Left drag rotate, right drag pan, mouse wheel zoom, WASD move.

Focus: Double-click a splat point to focus camera there.

Measure Mode: Enable Measure, single-click one splat point, then single-click another to create a measurement.

Measure Tools: Use Del Last to remove the latest measurement. Turning off Measure keeps existing measurements.

Tips: Click directly on the splat to place points. Dragging rotates the camera and does not create a measurement.
