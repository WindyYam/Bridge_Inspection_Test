Viewer Help

Camera: Left drag rotate, right drag pan, mouse wheel zoom, WASD move. On touch: one-finger drag rotate, two-finger drag pan, and pinch zoom.

Focus: Double-click or double-tap a scene point to focus camera there.

Measure Mode: Enable Measure, single-click one scene point, then single-click another to create a measurement.

Measure Tools: Use Del Last to remove the latest measurement. Turning off Measure keeps existing measurements.

Cut Plane: Enable Cut Plane, then drag a colored axis line to move along that axis, or drag the axis tip to rotate around that axis.

Tips: Click directly on the visible scene to place points. Dragging rotates the camera and does not create a measurement.
